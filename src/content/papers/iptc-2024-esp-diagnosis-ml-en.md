Paper ID: IPTC-24210

This paper evaluates various machine learning techniques for diagnosing Electrical Submersible Pump (ESP) events using synthetic time series data. The research demonstrates the effectiveness of ML approaches in identifying and classifying ESP operational issues, contributing to improved predictive maintenance strategies in the oil and gas industry.
