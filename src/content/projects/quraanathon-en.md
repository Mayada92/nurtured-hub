The Quraanathon hackathon project focused on creating a social application that serves the Holy Quran, with an emphasis on reflection and spiritual connection. The app combines technology with meaningful content to enhance users' engagement with religious teachings.

## Features

- Social features for community engagement
- Reflection tools and prompts
- Educational content
- User-friendly interface
