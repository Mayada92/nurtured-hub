**Patent Number:** US 8,988,475 B2  
**Publication Date:** March 24, 2015  
**Role:** Lead inventor

This patent describes the Blazer Printer, an innovative laser printer technology that enables inkless black-and-white printing through paper pyrolysis using lasers. The technology eliminates the need for traditional ink cartridges, offering an environmentally friendly alternative for printing applications.
